# Name Ideas for OpenClaw God Mode

## The Vibe
This isn't a friendly installer. It's a **guardrail remover** that gives an AI agent full root access. The name should reflect power, danger, or unrestricted capability — not "easy setup."

## 10 Options

### 1. **OpenClaw Unbound**
Suggests breaking free from restrictions. Clean, serious, describes exactly what it does.

### 2. **OpenClaw God Mode**
Gaming reference for unlimited power. Accurate but maybe too playful for a root-access tool.

### 3. **OpenClaw Raw**
Stripped of all safety layers. Short, punchy, implies "use at your own risk."

### 4. **OpenClaw RootShell**
Literal description of what the AI gets. Technically accurate, immediately clear to Linux users.

### 5. **OpenClaw Unleashed**
Released from default constraints. Sounds powerful without being overly edgy.

### 6. **OpenClaw Apex**
Top-tier, maximum capability install. Implies this is the "full power" version.

### 7. **OpenClaw Privileged**
References "privileged mode" / root access. Clean, technical, accurate.

### 8. **OpenClaw ZeroGuard**
Zero guardrails, zero prompts, zero restrictions. Gets the danger across immediately.

### 9. **OpenClaw Override**
Overrides all default safety settings. Sounds like you're bypassing something intentionally.

### 10. **OpenClaw Superuser**
Linux superuser (root) reference. Clear to the target audience.
